Is there anyone in the world that doesn't know the following?

  • When you shoot 8 frames per second, it is easy to get any facial/expression appearance you want; from lecherous to sad, from drunk to happy, from tired to wired.
  • A powerful flash can render apparently opaque clothing translucent.
